Ramen

Ingridients:

  • 2 packs of instant ramen noodles
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 boiled egg (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup spinach or bok choy (optional)
  • 1/4 cup corn kernels (optional)
  • Sriracha or chili oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water: In a medium pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Cook the noodles: Add the instant ramen noodles (without the seasoning packet) and cook for 2-3 minutes until tender. Drain and set aside.
  3. Make the broth: In the same pot, heat sesame oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, saut√©ing for 1 minute until fragrant. Add the mushrooms and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add liquids: Pour in 4 cups of water, soy sauce, and the ramen seasoning packets. Let it simmer for 5 minutes to develop the flavors.
  5. Add vegetables: Stir in spinach (or bok choy), green onions, and corn. Let them cook in the broth for 1-2 minutes until wilted.
  6. ServeDivide the cooked noodles into bowls. Pour the hot broth over the noodles, and top with a boiled egg and any extra toppings like sriracha or chili oil for heat.

Enjoy your homemade ramen!
