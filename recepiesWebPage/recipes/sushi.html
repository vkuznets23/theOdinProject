Sushi Rolls

Ingridients:

For the Sushi Rice:

  • 2 cups sushi rice (short-grain rice)
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For the Sushi Rolls:

  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 pound sushi-grade fish (e.g., tuna, salmon) or other fillings like cooked crab, shrimp, or tofu
  • Soy sauce (for dipping)
  • Pickled ginger (for serving)
  • Wasabi (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Sushi Rice: Rinse the rice until the water runs clear. Cook in a rice cooker or pot. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Fold into the cooked rice and let cool.
  2. Prepare the Fillings: Slice the cucumber, avocado, and fish (or other fillings) into thin strips.
  3. Assemble the Sushi Rolls: Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat. Spread rice over the nori, leaving an inch at the top. Lay fillings in a line and roll using the bamboo mat. Seal the edge with a bit of water.
  4. Slice and Serve: Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces. Serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi.

Enjoy your homemade sushi rolls!
